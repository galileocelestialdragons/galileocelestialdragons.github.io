Usually practice would consist of, meet up, warm up, cardio, strength training, water practice, and debrief.

After the team meets up at the benches, we would begin with a short warm up and then we’ll have a run together. After the run we would return back at the warm up spot and do some strength training or play some games. When it’s finally time to get in the boats, the team will change into their water practice gear, such as T-shirts and flip flops. Coaches will open up their cars as a place for paddlers to drop off their backpacks. Everyone will then head down to the dock and organized into boats.
